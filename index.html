3. Weekly Bonus

Valid Hours TargetValid Days TargetWeekly Bonus
10430%
Example

If a host earns 100K Rubies in one week (Sun 10:30 PM – Sun 10:30 PM), they receive a 30K Ruby bonus (30%), credited on Monday.

Bonus Requirements
  • Complete at least 4 active days
  • Stream a total of 10 hours in the week
Rules
  • Withdrawal Rate: 20,000 Ruby = 1 USD
  • Day Change: Daily at 10:30 PM
  • Weekly Period: Sunday 10:30 PM to Sunday 10:30 PM
  • Valid Day: Minimum 120 minutes (2 hours) Live
  • Valid Hour: 60+ minutes = 1 valid hour
  • Minimum Live Time: Sessions below 30 minutes are not counted
  • Bonus Payment: Every Monday (In Rubies)
  • Withdrawal: Once per day — Minimum $10 USD
Agency Change Rules
  • No live for 120 days
  • Current contract agency unreachable for 2 weeks
  • Both host and agency mutually agree

Strict Rule: Any misuse or cheating during transfer → Warning / Suspension / Ban

4.1 Week 5 and Beyond

Fixed Target Structure

From Week 5 onwards, the Ruby target is fixed at 300,000 Rubies per week, maintaining the $15 weekly guarantee.

Old Host Audition: If an existing/old host passes the Prestige Policy audition, they are placed directly at Week 5 — no ramp-up period.

Fixed Terms: 300,000 Ruby target · 4 days · 10 hrs/week

4.2 Requirements

4.3 Removal Triggers

• Not streaming regularly or bad attitude

• Not following team instructions
• Poor screen quality without fixing
• Breaking platform rules or laws
• 2 bans/week → removed from policy (1st ban: 1 day · 2nd: 2 days)
• Mandatory broadcast: 6 PM – 11:50 PM
• Old host with new ID → ban, no compensation
